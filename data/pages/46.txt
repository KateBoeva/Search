University policy is committed to affirmative action under law in employment of women, minority group members, individuals with disabilities, and protected veterans. Inquiries concerning these policies may be referred to Valarie Stanley, director of the Office for Equal Opportunity Programs, 221 Whitney Avenue, 3rd Floor, 203-432-0849. For additional information, see equalopportunity.yale.edu/. Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972 protects people from sex discrimination in educational programs and activities at institutions that receive federal financial assistance.
